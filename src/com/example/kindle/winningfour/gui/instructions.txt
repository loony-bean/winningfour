You and your opponent take turns putting pieces into the playing area using the 5-way selector. Press Left or Right to move selector and Down, Up or Select to put a new piece. To win you need to connect four pieces of your color vertically, horizontally or diagonally.

You can use game options to change the board size, choose computer or human to be your opponent or select who will make the first turn. Also you can adjust game timer and select graphical skin you like.

After the game is done press N or Select to start a new game.
